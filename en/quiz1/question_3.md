In JavaScript, you can use **properties** to change elements. 

Which of these properties allows you to insert new **tags** inside an element?

--- choices ---

- ( ) `innerText` 


  --- feedback ---

  --- /feedback ---

- (x) `innerHTML` 


  --- feedback ---

  Correct, this property let's you add new tags inside an element.

  --- /feedback ---

- ( ) `classList` 


  --- feedback ---
